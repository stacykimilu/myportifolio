PROJECT-1

EAT IT: Restaurant Recommendation and Reservation System

-The system was designed to help customers by suggesting restaurants of similar type, reservations according to price and ratings within the vicinity of the application user. The three main actors are the customers, the administrator and the restaurant owner. The customer logins into the system. The customer receives a list of restaurants that are according to his budget. They pick the restaurant and begin to enter the details, date and time of reservation of the table space and confirm the reservation. The administrator will be able to add users and add the reservation details. The client receives a notification that the reservation has been received. The restaurant owner will be able to accept and view the reservation that have already been made. The main deliverable in our case is to create and provide the application to be used by each and every individual. This system will enable customers to reserve table space according to their convenience Language used is php and java
